hulk_recoil
(/obj/item/bodypart/arm, /mob/living/carbon/human/hulkman, damage)

Deals damage back to the hulk's arm.

When a hulk manages to break a wall using their hulk smash, this deals back damage to the arm used. This is in its own proc just to be easily overridden by other wall types. Default allows for three smashed walls per arm. Arguments: *arg1 is the arm to deal damage to. *arg2 is the hulk
